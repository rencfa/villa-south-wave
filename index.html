Villa South Wave

Toiny, Saint-Barthélemy

A Sanctuary Facing the Atlantic

Discover Villa South Wave, an exceptional retreat nestled in the heart of the wild and authentic Toiny district. This unique residence was designed for those seeking to escape the daily hustle and reconnect with the raw nature of Saint-Barthélemy. Facing the vastness of the ocean, the villa offers a permanent spectacle where the azure blue of the sky meets the Atlantic waves.

The villa features two elegant suites, each with its own private bathroom, offering absolute comfort and total privacy for four guests. The living areas are bathed in light: a spacious lounge and a modern kitchen, both fully air-conditioned, open onto a large wooden terrace. This is where the magic happens, between the heated pool that seems to merge with the horizon and the shaded relaxation areas.

To complete your experience, the villa is equipped with a tropical outdoor shower, ideal after a surf session or a walk on Toiny beach, accessible in just a few minutes on foot. Whether you are a fan of contemporary design or a lover of West Indian tranquility, Villa South Wave is the promise of an unforgettable stay, where luxury is combined with simplicity and elegance.
